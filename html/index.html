The VISION AVTR

Inspered by the future

Concept

Electric mobility in harmony with man and nature. Eco-friendly technology of organic batteries and a well-thought-out operational strategy of the VISION AVTR concept car.

Premier

A new concept Mercedes-Benz - VISION AVTR was presented at CES 2020. The futuristic concept of the presented electric car unites man, nature and the car in a unique way.

Ola Kallenius, Chairman of the Management Board of Daimler AG and Mercedes-Benz AG, in his speech presented a concept car together with Oscar winner and creator of the movie Avatar, James Cameron.

It was the philosophy created by the cult director in a fantastic film that became the basis for the creation of VISION AVTR.

"Mercedes-Benz has always been one of the most technologically advanced premium brands. Now it's time to combine luxury and environmental friendliness. After all, this is the only way we can meet the standards of the future."

INTERIOR & EXTERIOR

VISION AVTR design is the epitome of environmental friendliness

The innovative VISION AVTR concept combines the eco-friendly interaction of interior design, exterior and UX. The whole design process was focused on a specific result – a unique experience of interaction and perception of the concept car by the driver and passengers. It was about creating a unique space in which passengers have a biometric connection with each other, with the vehicle and the surrounding world.

The lateral outer opening passes through the inner part and creates an endless loop, the prototype of which was the sacred connection between the Na'vi people in the Avatar movie and their natural habitat.

Also, thanks to the elongated "One Bow" design and organic design philosophy, VISION AVTR demonstrates a radically new stage of the automotive industry. Thus, having gone from the concept of awareness to a unique fusion of a person and a car, Mercedes-Benz engineers managed to create a VISION AVTR concept electric car.

The laconic continuation of the minimalistic exterior design is the front seats, made in a very organic form that resembles leafy hammocks from the planet Pandora. The center console symbolizes the Tree of Souls, the most sacred place of the Na'vi. The control unit is intuitive and incredibly functional.

In addition, the control center is located in such a way that it is easily accessible for both the driver and the passenger, as well as for passengers in the rear.

Thus, VISION AVTR establishes a close biometric connection with the driver and helps to raise awareness of the surrounding situation.

The back of the seats and the interior of the roof are made of a special fabric that changes color. Depending on the lighting, textiles can be dark blue or a delicate light blue color.

But in addition, the environmental friendliness of the electric car interior is enhanced with the help of DINAMICA® vegetarian leather – the first and only microfiber that guarantees environmental efficiency throughout the entire production cycle.

DINAMICA® is a recycled material from old clothes, fabrics and plastic PET bottles.
